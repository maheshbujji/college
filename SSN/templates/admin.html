SSN is dedicated to imparting futuristic technical and management education in the key academic-industrial areas and instill higher standards of knowledge to students through qualified faculty and excellent infrastructure set to global standards, thus improving the quality of technical education and contributing to the development of the Nation.
Established in the year 2001 at Ongole, Prakasam district, Andhra Pradesh.
Sri Satyanarayana Engineering college is considered to be one of the best and finest colleges that provide professional education to students in AP. Every year thousands of fresh professionals get introduced to the industry by SSN.
With the Wifi college campus spread across 2 acres, students find it the best place to study professional courses.

The annual tuition fee fixed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh(AFRC) for B.Tech courses in this college for the academic year 2016-17,2017-2018,and 2018-2019 is Rs.50,200 (Fifty Thousand two hundred ).
Hence, the candidates taking admission in this college are to give an undertaking that they will pay the difference of tuition fees, if any.
The tuition fee is same for both Category – A Seats and Category – B.Seats.


The main difference between Category – A Seats and Category – B seats is, B – CATEGORY STUDENTS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE FOR TUITION FEE REIMBURSEMENT OR ANY WELFARE SCHOLARSHIPS.
